Guidelines for Posting

Name and Email

Please enter your name (first or first and last) and your email so you can let others associate your post with you. Please make sure that you use the same Mac email that you used to create your account!

Title

Add a good and informative and descriptive title.

Price

You only have to enter a number, no need to add a dollar sign.

Description

Tell us a little bit about your item you are trying to get rid of, don't be too wordy as there is a 500 character limit.

Tags

Chose a tag that correlates to what you're trying to get rid of. This will help other users to filter posts and find what they are searching for.

Image

Please upload one clear image that shows what you are looking to sell or trade. Must be a .jpg or .png file!

One last Note

Remember to be respectful to buyers and do your best to communicate with them in a timely manner. Good luck getting your item gone!
