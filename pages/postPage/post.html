Guidelines for Posting

Name and Email

Please enter your name (first or first and last) and your email so you can let others associate your post with you.

Title

Add a good and informative title to inform or draw others intentions

Price

For this field you only have to put in a number, no need to add a dollar sign.

Description

Tell us a little bit about your item you are trying to get rid of, don't be too wordy as there is a 500 character limit.

Image

Please upload one clear image that shows what you are looking to sell or trade.

One last Note

Remember to be respectful to buyers and do your best to communicate with them in a timely manner. Good luck getting your item gone!
